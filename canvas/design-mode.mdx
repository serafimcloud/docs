---
title: Design Mode
description: Visual no-code editor for styling components
---

## When to use

Use Design Mode for quick visual tweaks: colors, spacing, typography, and effects. Use AI chat for larger structural changes.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a component">
    Click on the component you want to edit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Design Mode">
    Press `D` or open the Design tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Inspect elements">
    Click elements in the preview to inspect them
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust styles">
    Use the controls to modify:
    - Colors & backgrounds
    - Typography
    - Spacing (padding & margins)
    - Effects (borders & shadows)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Save your changes when done
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

<Tip>
**Save regularly** — Don't lose your tweaks!
</Tip>

<Tip>
**Make small adjustments** — Iterate incrementally for best results.
</Tip>
