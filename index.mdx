## Community: components for AI agents

21st is a **Community** library of open-source React/Tailwind components, real app screens, and themes that you can use with any AI coding assistant (Cursor, Claude Code, Lovable, etc.).

- Copy components and drop them into your repo via your editor
- Paste prompts into AI coding assistants like Cursor, Claude Code, Lovable, or others to generate variations in their environments
- Browse production-ready components, real app screens, and themes

## Who is 21st for?

- **Frontend engineers** — Find and use high-quality React/Tailwind components to speed up your workflow.
- **Designers** — Explore real app screens and themes for inspiration and reference.
- **Product teams & founders** — Use community components as building blocks for your projects.

## What makes 21st different?

- **Community of real components** — Not just screenshots: installable, remixable React code.
- **Works with any AI assistant** — Use component prompts in Cursor, Claude Code, Lovable, or any other tool.
- **Production-ready code** — Components are built with best practices and ready to ship.
