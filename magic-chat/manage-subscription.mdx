## View Your Current Plan

Go to **Settings** → **Billing** to see:

- Your current plan and credits usage
- Next payment date
- Account balance
- Invoice history

You can also see your remaining credits in the **Magic Chat sidebar**.

## Change Your Plan

To upgrade or switch plans:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade** and choose a new plan
3. Complete checkout via Stripe

When upgrading mid-cycle, you'll receive prorated credit for the remaining time on your current plan.

## Manage Payment Method

Click **Manage Subscription** in **Settings** → **Billing** to open the Stripe Customer Portal, where you can:

- Update your payment method
- Change your billing address
- View and download invoices

## Cancel Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open billing settings">
    Go to **Settings** → **Billing** in your 21st account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel your plan">
    Click **Cancel subscription** at the bottom of the billing page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep access until period ends">
    Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Downgrade to Free">
    After the billing period ends, your account automatically switches to the Free plan with 100 monthly credits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can also cancel via the **Stripe Customer Portal** by clicking **Manage Subscription** in billing settings.
</Note>

### What Happens When You Cancel

- **Immediate**: Your subscription is marked for cancellation at the end of the billing period
- **Until period ends**: You keep full access to your paid plan and remaining credits
- **After period ends**: Your account moves to the Free plan (100 credits/month), usage resets to zero
- **Your projects are preserved**: All your Magic Chat projects and generated code remain accessible

### Resubscribe

You can resubscribe at any time by going to **Settings** → **Billing** and choosing a new plan. Your previous projects will still be available.
