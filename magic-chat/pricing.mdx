Magic Chat uses a **credit-based** system. Every AI interaction consumes credits from your monthly allowance.

## Plans

| Plan | Monthly Credits | Price (monthly) | Price (yearly) |
|------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| **Free** | 100 | $0 | $0 |
| **Pro** | 400 | $20/mo | $16/mo (billed $192/yr) |
| **Pro Plus** | 200 | $40/mo | $32/mo (billed $384/yr) |
| **Max** | 2,000 | $100/mo | $67/mo (billed $804/yr) |

<Note>
Pro Plus includes early access to new features. Max includes priority support.
</Note>

## What's Included in Every Plan

All plans (including Free) include:

- **Magic Chat** — AI-powered component generation
- **Magic MCP** — Use 21st components from your IDE
- **UI Inspiration Library** — Unlimited browsing
- **SVG Logo Search** — Unlimited searches
- **Community support** via Discord

## Pro-Only Features

- **Site Clone** — Recreate any website's design from a URL
- **Email support** (Pro), **Priority support** (Pro Plus, Max)

## How Credits Work

Each AI interaction in Magic Chat or Magic MCP consumes credits from your monthly allowance. Credits reset at the start of each billing cycle.

- On the **Free** plan, you get 100 credits per month with usage-based pricing
- On **paid plans**, your credits are included in the subscription price

You can check your current usage at any time in **Settings** → **Billing**, or in the Magic Chat sidebar.

## Upgrade Your Plan

1. Go to [21st.dev/pricing](https://21st.dev/pricing), or
2. Open **Settings** → **Billing** in the app, or
3. Click **Upgrade** in the Magic Chat sidebar

Select your desired plan and complete checkout via Stripe.
