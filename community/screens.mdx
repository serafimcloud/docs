## Viewing Screens

### App Pages

Click any app card to see all captured screens from that application:
- Full page screenshots
- Individual UI components
- Different app states and views

### Preview Dialog

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open preview">
    Click any screen thumbnail
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch views">
    Press `Tab` to toggle between:
    - **All Components** — UI elements from this screen
    - **Full Page** — Complete page screenshot
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Use `←` and `→` to browse through screens
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bookmark">
    Press `B` to save for later
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Screens

### For Inspiration

Browse screens to find design patterns and UI ideas. See how top companies solve common design problems.

### With AI Tools

Reference screens when prompting AI:

1. Bookmark apps you like at [21st.dev/community/screens](https://21st.dev/community/screens)
2. In [Canvas Plan mode](/canvas/plan-mode), the AI uses your bookmarked apps to understand your design preferences
3. Get implementations that match your taste

<Tip>
**Bookmark your favorites** — The more apps you bookmark, the better AI understands your design style.
</Tip>

### Add to Canvas

When using Canvas, press `Enter` in the preview dialog to add a screen directly to your canvas as a reference.

## Managing Bookmarks

Press `B` on any screen or app to bookmark it. Access all saved screens from **Bookmarks → Screens** in the sidebar.
